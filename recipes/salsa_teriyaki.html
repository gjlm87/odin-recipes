Salsa Teriyaki

Ingredients

  • 1/2 garlic.
  • 75 ml. soy sauce.
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar.
  • 2 tbsp. brown sugar.
  • 2 teaspoons grated ginger.
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch.
  • 1 tablespoon of water.
  • Sesame oil.

Steps

  1. The first thing we are going to do is to chop half a clove of garlic. Set aside.
  2. Then, we are going to put in a pot the soy sauce, a tablespoon of rice vinegar, two tablespoons of brown sugar.
  3. Add the garlic and also some grated ginger.
  4. Add a tablespoon of cornstarch, a splash of lemon juice and a splash of sesame oil.
  5. Bring the pot to the fire until the cornstarch acts and the mixture thickens.
  6. Once the consistency changes, remove it and take it to the container in which it will be stored.
