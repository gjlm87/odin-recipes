Arepas Venezolanas

Arepas Venezuelan

Ingredients

  • 3 cups warm or hot water
  • 2 cups of precooked corn flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tsp. olive oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl place the water together with the salt and the olive oil. Mix and add the flour a little at a time while continuing to stir.
  2. Once all the dough is incorporated, start mixing with your hands while the flour absorbs the liquid. If necessary add a little more flour and the final texture should be similar to a moldable mashed potato. When the desired consistency is reached, let stand for 10 minutes.
  3. After this time, with the help of your hands grab balls of the dough to start shaping them rounded and then flat. Repeat this process with all the dough.
  4. Heat a frying pan with a little oil in the base and when it is hot place one or two arepas at a time. Cook over medium heat for 5-7 minutes.
  5. After this time turn them over and cook for 10 more minutes. Then repeat the cooking process with all the dough.
  6. They will be ready to fill and eat.
